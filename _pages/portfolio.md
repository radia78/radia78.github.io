[**Infected Leaf Vein Segmentation**](https://github.com/radia78/rotten-tomatoes-mlm24) (December 2024)


The project was a part of UW-Madison's ML+X machine learning hackathon. Our team placed 1st by yielding the highest test IOU. Our approach was to recursively refine coarse predictions from a U-Net instead of focusing on better features.

[**Updating Transformers**](https://github.com/radia78/Transformer2) (December 2023)


During GPT-mania, I decided to better understand the architectural effects of the transformers by just training one. I modified the original Encoder-Decoder model with RoPE, RMSNorm, and SwiGLU activation.
