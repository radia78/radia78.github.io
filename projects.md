### Transformers 2
LLAMA 2.0 introduced new architectural changes to the transformers model such as Rotary Positional Embedding, SwiGLU activation, and RMSNorm. The project evaluates the effect of the components on the original transformer's performance in machine translation. The components shows a 3% increase in the BLEU score from the original transformer while decreasing the size of the model by 3%.
